Philip Lindsay, Libertarian, Veteran, Father.

Philip Lindsay is a 32 year old father of three. He is married to Sarah Lindsay for 14 years in September 2020. He is from Traverse City, MI and graduated in 2005 from Central High School. Currently is attending NMC for a degree in Computer Information Technology.

Philip, or Phil as his friends prefer, enlisted in 2005 in the Army and deployed twice to Iraq. He was stationed at Fort Knox, Fort Bliss, Fort Sill, Fort Riley, and did a year tour in Camp Casey in South Korea. He was medically discharged after 13 years and now lives in SOuth Boardman, MI where he is actively a member of the Northwest Michigan Libertarian Party affiliate.
